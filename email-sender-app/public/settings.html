Update Email Settings

Instructions to set up Gmail OAuth2 for sending emails:
  1. Go to the Google Cloud Console.
  2. Create a new project or select an existing one.
  3. Go to "APIs & Services" > "Library", search for "Gmail API", and click "Enable".
  4. Go to "APIs & Services" > "Credentials", click "Create Credentials" dropdown > "OAuth 2.0 Client ID", select "Web application", set a name, and add https://developers.google.com/oauthplayground as an authorized redirect URI.
  5. Go to "APIs & Services" > "OAuth consent screen", under "Audience", scroll down to "Test users", click "Add users", enter your Gmail address, and save.
  6. Copy the Client ID and Client Secret from the credentials.
  7. Go to OAuth 2.0 Playground.
  8. Click the gear icon, check "Use your own OAuth credentials", and enter your Client ID and Secret.
  9. In the "Input your own scopes" box, enter: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send
  10. Click "Authorize APIs" and authorize access.
  11. Click "Exchange authorization code for tokens" and copy the Refresh Token.
  12. Enter your Gmail address, Client ID, Client Secret, and Refresh Token below.

Note: Each user must configure their own Gmail OAuth2 credentials. The app uses these to send emails from your Gmail account.
